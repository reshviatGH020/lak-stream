About us

Customize Everything With Elementor

LAKStream gives you unmatched control with Elementor, the world’s most popular WordPress page builder. From layout design to typography, every element is easily customizable through a simple drag-and-drop interface, no coding skills required. Whether you're creating landing pages, homepage sliders, or dynamic content sections, Elementor lets you build it visually and in real time. Use over 100 pre-designed modules to accelerate your workflow, all while maintaining design consistency with global color palettes and font settings. You can rearrange video blocks, embed content, build carousels, and integrate custom HTML or widgets.

Responsive Design For All Devices

In today’s digital world, your audience accesses content from a wide variety of devices like desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. That’s why LAKStream is built with full responsiveness in mind, ensuring your OTT platform looks amazing and functions smoothly across all screen sizes. Whether it’s a 6-inch mobile screen or a 4K ultra-wide monitor, the layout automatically adapts to deliver the best viewing experience. Fonts resize fluidly, video players adjust proportionally, and navigation remains intuitive no matter the device. Unlike other themes that struggle with mobile layout issues, LAKStream uses modern CSS grid and flexbox techniques to optimize every component for multi-device compatibility.
